Rules:
If you try to move outside the bounds of the board you lose.
If you move the head into the snake's body you lose.

User Controls:
Use arrow keys to move up/down and left/right.
Game settings:
Choose the predefined board size, the bigger the easier.
Choose the difficulty, this will influence the snake's speed, the harder the faster.
Leaderboard:
When done playing enter the handle under which you want so save your score.
The top 5 scores get displayed on the leaderboard so give your best and you might be among the champions!
